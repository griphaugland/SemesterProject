What do we research?

CANCER

Led by Dr. Harry Minset

Our research is all about finding methods to prevent cancer from taking any more lives.

We have a team of 5 scientists and doctors led by the experienced and decorated Dr. Minset.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Led by Michelle Turner

The possibilites that Artificial intelligence brings are endless. Imagine how far human kind could come with machines 100 times smarter than us on our side.

Our AI team is led by experienced computer science expert Michelle Turner.

ASTROPHYSICS

Led by Dr. George Michaels

Getting an understanding of the universe is something that is immensely important

Follow the journey of our solar exploration: from early Arab traders, to Galileo’s telescope, to the latest exploration of the planets in our solar system.
