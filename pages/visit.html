LOCATION

We are located at
Bredalsmarken 15,
5006 Bergen
Norway

Its a 5-10 minute walk from Bergen city center.

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

PARKING

There is several places to park nearby. We offer free parking right outside our museum. As there is only 25 available parking spots it might be smart to be early!

If there is nothing available here there is always available spaces at the various parking houses in Bergen.

We also have 4 parking spots reserved for visitors with handicap permits.

ADMISSION

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

SHOPS

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Our souvenir shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
